Nine Things to do in Your First Two Weeks in University

  • Get: your university ID card, the rules handbook, your schedule. Get familiar with the campus. Also, find out how to use your university card/library card for finding articles, books, resources, a public transit student card.
  • Locate a computer that you can use for school work.
  • Find out what school supplies are required and the location of stores with the best prices
  • Locate the public library and the community agency closest to your home.
  • Learn what help is available to students in your school.
  • Get maps of city and public transit routes
